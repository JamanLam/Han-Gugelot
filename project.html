The shape of the radius is rectangular. It has a half meter metal front and its side parts are made of walnut wood. The purpose of the wood was that the new apparatus would not be excessively modern.

The design focused on functionality, simplicity, and user-centered design. This philosophy emphasized a minimalist approach to create aesthetically pleasing products that were easy to use and understand.

After finalizing the design and selecting the materials, the SK4 went into production. Braun employed advanced manufacturing techniques to ensure the quality and consistency of the final product.
